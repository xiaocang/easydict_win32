# Privacy Policy for Easydict Win32

**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Overview

Easydict Win32 is an open-source translation dictionary application for Windows. This privacy policy explains how the application handles your data.

## Data Collection

**Easydict Win32 does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us.** The application runs entirely on your local device.

## Third-Party Translation Services

Easydict Win32 integrates with various third-party translation services to provide translation functionality. When you use these services, the text you submit for translation is sent to the respective service provider:

- **Google Translate** - Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **DeepL** - Subject to [DeepL's Privacy Policy](https://www.deepl.com/privacy)
- **OpenAI** - Subject to [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- **Google Gemini** - Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **DeepSeek** - Subject to [DeepSeek's Privacy Policy](https://www.deepseek.com/privacy)
- **Groq** - Subject to [Groq's Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy)

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any translation service you choose to use.

## Local Data Storage

The following data is stored **locally on your device only**:

- **API Keys**: Any API keys you configure for translation services are stored locally in your application settings. These keys are never transmitted to us.
- **Application Settings**: Your preferences (theme, hotkeys, window settings, etc.) are stored locally.
- **No Cloud Sync**: Your data is not synced to any cloud service by this application.

## Clipboard Access

Easydict Win32 may access your clipboard when you use the clipboard monitoring feature. This data is processed locally and is only sent to translation services when you explicitly request a translation.

## Network Communication

The application only communicates with:
1. The translation service APIs you choose to use
2. No analytics, telemetry, or tracking services

## Data Security

- API keys are stored in local application data
- No data is transmitted to third parties except the translation services you explicitly use
- All communication with translation services uses HTTPS encryption

## Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
